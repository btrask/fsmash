    About

    This website, fsmash.org, is a simple Wi-Fi friend finder for the game Super Smash Bros. Brawl. Please read the first link below for general information.

    Rules
    1. Don’t spam.
    2. Keep discussions on topic for the channel you are in.
    3. Limit one account per person and per IP address (unless you get special permission).
    4. Don’t try to cheat the rating system.
    5. Don’t ask your opponents to rate you after you play. (Asking before you play is fine.)

    I’m trying to keep this list as minimal as possible. Don’t make me expand it.

    Trolling is allowed as long as it follows the other rules.

    If you believe someone has broken the rules, please press the Report… button in the channel where it happened. No action will be taken immediately, but the last 50 messages in the channel will be saved for review. Please don’t abuse this feature.

    If you submit a video that isn’t related to Brawl (or one that’s really bad), it will probably get deleted. No machinima.

    If you need a second user account for a legitimate reason (e.g. for a sibling), please contact me.

    Bans

    All bans are permanent by default. However, if you believe you were unjustly banned, please contact me on SWF with a full explanation.

    Proxies are not allowed. You may be banned for using one. If you have a legitimate reason for needing one, please contact me.

    Subscribe

    This website has an ongoing cost of time and money to run. In order to keep the quality of the site high, please consider contributing.

    I've put together a new subscription system. A $4 donation earns a 3 month subscription. You can donate any amount you wish ($4 minimum), with a proportional subscription length.

    Thank you! to everyone who helps out. --Ben

    Benefits

    Subscribers are thanked with the "Subscriber" title, and may choose a custom color for their name in chat messages, in addition to a small icon that is also added for them.

    Subscribers may ask to have their usernames changed. Name changes will be granted infrequently (about once per year per person) and on a case-by-case basis. Usernames will no longer be changed for non-subscribers.

    In the future, subscribers will be able to host tournaments using online bracket software that is currently in development. (This feature is not yet complete and subject to change.)

    Fine Print

    Subscribing does not grant any special rights or privileges. You must still follow all of the site rules. If you are banned, your subscription fee will not be refunded.

    If you have donated at any time in the past, your donation will automatically be credited at a rate of $5/mo. If you donated, but for some reason you weren’t credited, please contact me.

    The minimum donation is $4. Smaller amounts are appreciated, but will not earn subscriber status.
